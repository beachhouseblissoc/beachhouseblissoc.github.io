About Us

Hello, I'm Terri

I live near Bolsa Chica State Beach and Huntington Harbour, and my home is exactly the kind of place dogs love — warm, open, and full of life.

I take one dog at a time, unless authorized by the owners. Your pup gets the full run of my home, my secure patio, and my attention for every moment of their stay.

Daily walks, feedings and medications on your schedule, and flexible timing so travel is stress-free for both of you.

This is a place where you can have your dog stay, be cared for, loved, while you are away, and never be worried.

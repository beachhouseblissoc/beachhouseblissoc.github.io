About Us

Hi, I'm Terri — and this is Plum

We live near Bolsa Chica State Beach and Huntington Harbour, and our home is exactly the kind of place dogs love — warm, open, and full of life.

We take one dog at a time, unless authorized by the owners. Your pup gets the full run of our home, our secure patio, and our attention for every moment of their stay.

Daily walks, feedings and medications on your schedule, and flexible timing so travel is stress-free for both of you.
